Specialized Korean Translator

  • Home
  • Career
  • Specialized Korean Translator

Job Summary

We are looking for a Korean translator to join our creative and professional team. He/she will be responsible for delivering high quality and accurate translations to and from the Korean language, while ensuring the appropriate meaning, style, and cultural nuances of the source language (SL).

Job Responsibilities

  • Accurately and professionally translate documents to and from the Korean language.
  • Review and edit translated documents to ensure high QA standards and avoidance of any grammatical and stylistic mistakes.
  • Cooperate with editing and marketing teams to ensure consistency across all translated materials.
  • Work on interpreting projects when needed.
  • Ensure the accuracy of technical and cultural terminologies.
  • Effectively manage and coordinate the projects deadlines.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor degree in translation, languages or any related field.
  • Proven track record of +2 years in translating to and from the Korean language.
  • Full mastery of the spoken and written Korean language.
  • Full command of the grammar and syntax of the SL and TL.
  • Ability to effectively and seamlessly work on various CAT tools.
  • Have strong analytical skills and attention to details.
  • Handle work pressure and tight deadlines.
